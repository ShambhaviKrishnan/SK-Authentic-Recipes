Questions & Answers

Can I upload my own recipes?
Yes, you can upload your recipes!
Are there non-vegetarian dishes as well?
No, I strictly make vegetarian dishes.
How do I save my favorite recipes?
You can save your favorite recipes by clicking the "Save" button on each recipe page.
Can I share recipes with my friends?
Yes, you can share recipes via social media platforms or by copying the recipe link.
Is there a mobile app for SK Authentic Recipes?
Currently, we are working on our mobile app, which will be available soon!
What should I do if I find an error in a recipe?
If you find an error, you can report it to us via the "Report" button on the recipe page or contact us directly.
