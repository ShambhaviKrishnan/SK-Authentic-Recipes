Idli with Chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Idli batter
  • 1 cup Coconut, grated
  • 1 Green chili
  • 1/2 inch Ginger
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Instructions

1. Prepare idli batter and pour into idli molds.

2. Steam for about 10-12 minutes until cooked.

3. For chutney, blend coconut, green chili, ginger, and salt until smooth.

4. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, and pour over chutney.

5. Serve idlis hot with chutney.
