Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer, cubed
  • 2 cups Spinach (Palak)
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Instructions

1. Blanch the spinach in boiling water, then cool and puree.

2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.

3. Add onions and saut√© until golden.

4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste, then add tomato puree and cook.

5. Add spinach puree, salt, and garam masala; mix well.

6. Add paneer cubes and simmer for a few minutes. Serve hot!
