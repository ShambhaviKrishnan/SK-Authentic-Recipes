Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Milk powder
  • 1/4 cup All-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1/2 tsp Rose water

Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda.

2. Gradually add milk to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 10 minutes.

3. In a pan, combine sugar and water; bring to a boil to make syrup.

4. Shape dough into small balls and fry in hot oil until golden brown.

5. Soak fried balls in sugar syrup for 30 minutes. Serve warm.
