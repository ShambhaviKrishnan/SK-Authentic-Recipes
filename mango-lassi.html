Mango Lassi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 ripe Mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2-3 tbsp Sugar (to taste)
  • Ice cubes
  • A pinch of cardamom powder

Instructions

1. In a blender, combine yogurt, mango, milk, sugar, and cardamom.

2. Blend until smooth and creamy.

3. Add ice cubes and blend again.

4. Serve chilled. Enjoy your refreshing mango lassi!
