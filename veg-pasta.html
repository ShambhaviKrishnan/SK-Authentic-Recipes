Veg Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta (any type)
  • 1 cup Mixed vegetables (bell peppers, peas, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tsp Italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for garnish (optional)

Instructions

1. Boil pasta as per package instructions; drain and set aside.

2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© mixed vegetables.

3. Add the boiled pasta, herbs, salt, and pepper.

4. Toss everything together and cook for a few minutes.

5. Serve hot, garnished with Parmesan cheese.
