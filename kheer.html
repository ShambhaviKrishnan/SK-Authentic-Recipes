Kheer

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 4 cups Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Nuts (Almonds, Cashews)
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder
  • Raisins (optional)

Instructions

1. Rinse the rice and soak for 30 minutes.

2. Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan.

3. Add soaked rice to the boiling milk and cook on low flame.

4. Stir occasionally until rice is soft and kheer thickens.

5. Add sugar, nuts, and cardamom powder; mix well.

6. Serve warm or chilled.
