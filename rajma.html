Rajma

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Kidney beans (Rajma)
  • 2 Onions, finely chopped
  • 2 Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 Green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Instructions

1. Soak the kidney beans overnight and cook them until soft.

2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.

3. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.

4. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cooking for a minute.

5. Add the tomato puree, salt, and cook until the oil separates.

6. Add the cooked kidney beans and some water; simmer for 10-15 minutes.

7. Sprinkle garam masala before serving. Enjoy with rice!
