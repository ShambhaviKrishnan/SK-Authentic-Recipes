Dosa with Sambar

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Dosa batter
  • 1 cup Toor dal
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 2 Green chilies, slit
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Instructions

1. Cook toor dal in water until soft; mash and set aside.

2. In a pot, heat oil, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.

3. Add onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and saut√© until soft.

4. Add cooked dal, salt, and water; simmer for 10 minutes.

5. Prepare dosas on a hot griddle until golden brown.

6. Serve dosas hot with sambar and chutney.
