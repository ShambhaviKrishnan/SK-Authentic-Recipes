Veg Hakka Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g Hakka noodles
  • 1 cup Mixed vegetables (carrots, capsicum, beans)
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Chili sauce
  • 2 Green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Instructions

1. Boil the Hakka noodles as per package instructions and drain.

2. Heat oil in a pan, add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.

3. Add mixed vegetables and saut√© for a few minutes.

4. Stir in the boiled noodles, soy sauce, chili sauce, and salt.

5. Toss everything together and serve hot.
